We recommend professional window cleaning at least twice a year for residential properties and monthly for commercial properties. Pressure washing uses high-pressure water (1,500-4,000 PSI) for tough surfaces like driveways and concrete. Soft washing uses low pressure (under 500 PSI) with specialized cleaning solutions for delicate surfaces like roofs, siding, and painted areas. We'll recommend the best method for your specific needs.
